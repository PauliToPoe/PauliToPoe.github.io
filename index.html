Pauli To Poe

About

Hello fellow humans and literate animals. This website was borne from the mind of a Master's physics student looking for expression. Physics, with all it's magnificent beauty, is a different kind of beauty to that found in literature which seeks to convey beauty through illustriuos descriptions, through events in a story that evoke or perhaps through the altered lens that you see the universe through after a good book. This kind of beauty, is what makes people individual. I have spent too long with physics as my sole source of beauty; countless manipulations of Pauli matrices. It is time I taste the other kind of beauty. So, welcome to my website, if anything is useful to you in someway I am grateful, join me on my journey from Pauli to Poe.
